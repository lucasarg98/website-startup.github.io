Defining the Next Era of Aerial Defense

At Zylos, we design and manufacture next-generation autonomous tactical UAVs engineered for high-stakes defense and reconnaissance operations. Born from the intersection of advanced aerospace engineering and tactical artificial intelligence.

Our drones are sophisticated, self-contained tactical assets capable of real-time threat evaluation and flawless execution under electronic warfare conditions.
